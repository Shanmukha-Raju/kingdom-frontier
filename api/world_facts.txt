Kingdom Frontier is a medieval fantasy realm nestled between the Thornwall Mountains and the Azure Coast.
The capital city, Ironhaven, serves as the central hub where adventurers gather to seek quests and fortune.
The Market Square in Ironhaven is a bustling trade center where Mira the Merchant sells weapons, potions, and supplies.
The Castle Gate is the only passage into the inner keep, guarded day and night by Captain Aldric and his Royal Guard.
The Sacred Grove lies at the forest's edge, where Elder Thorn communes with ancient spirits and nature's wisdom.
The Dockside Tavern is a shadowy establishment where the Shadow Guild conducts covert dealings through their agent known only as Shade.
The player is a newly arrived adventurer seeking to prove their worth and gain entry to the King's court.
To enter the inner keep, Captain Aldric requires the player to prove themselves by slaying the terrifying Red Dragon in the mountains.
Elder Thorn in the Sacred Grove can open a magical portal to the Dragon's Lair, but only after Captain Aldric suggests that the player meet him.
The Red Dragon shoots deadly fireballs and can only be slain using weapons like the Frostbane Katana or the Diamond Greatsword.
Slaying the dragon yields a precious Dragon Diamond as proof of the deed.
Captain Aldric will consume the Dragon Diamond as proof to grant entry to the Castle Interior.
Inside the Castle Interior, the player can loot royal throne treasures like Golden Chalices, Crown Jewels, and Royal Scepters.
Shade at the Dockside Tavern will buy the Dragon Diamond and Castle Loot for a premium price (75% of base price instead of 50%).
Shade can forge the Diamond Greatsword (deals 20 damage, 15 durability) in exchange for 1 Dragon Diamond and 3 Raw Meats.
Every time the player exits the castle, Captain Aldric locks the gate and demands a new Dragon Diamond for re-entry.
Health Potions bought from Mira heal 50 HP, and Raw Meat obtained from hunting wild boars heals 3 to 5 HP.
