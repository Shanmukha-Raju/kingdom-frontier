Kingdom Frontier is a medieval fantasy realm nestled between the Thornwall Mountains and the Azure Coast.
The capital city, Ironhaven, serves as the central hub where adventurers gather to seek quests and fortune.
The Market Square in Ironhaven is a bustling trade center where Mira the Merchant sells weapons, potions, and supplies.
The Castle Gate is the only passage into the inner keep, guarded day and night by Captain Aldric and his Royal Guard.
The Sacred Grove lies at the forest's edge, where Elder Thorn communes with ancient spirits and nature's wisdom.
The Dockside Tavern is a shadowy establishment where the Shadow Guild conducts covert dealings through their agent known only as Shade.
The player is a newly arrived adventurer seeking to prove their worth and gain entry to the King's court.
To enter the inner keep, an adventurer must earn the trust of Captain Aldric by completing a worthy deed.
Mira the Merchant sells a legendary katana called Frostbane for 8 gold, which is said to impress even the Royal Guard.
Gold coins can be found scattered in hidden places around Ironhaven — behind crates in the market, near the old well, and in the forest clearing.
The Shadow Guild operates in secret, offering lucrative but morally questionable missions to those who prove their discretion.
Elder Thorn knows the location of an ancient relic hidden deep in the Thornwall Mountains, but only shares this knowledge with those he trusts.
The Royal Guard protects the kingdom from threats beyond the Thornwall Mountains, where dark creatures stir.
The Forest Wardens are an ancient order dedicated to preserving the balance between civilization and the wild.
There are rumors of a dragon awakening in the Thornwall Mountains, and the King seeks brave adventurers to investigate.
