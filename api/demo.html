KINGDOM FRONTIER

THE LORE OF KINGDOM FRONTIER

The northern frontier of the kingdom is cold, wild, and forgotten. Dark rumors whisper that a shard of Frostbane—a mythical relic sword of ancient power—lies frozen somewhere in these snowfields. As a newly arrived adventurer, you must navigate local factions, complete tasks, trade, and build relationships to uncover the location of the shard. Approach the frontier's inhabitants to uncover the secrets!
Mira
Merchant
Handles the Market Square. Sells tools, items, and values gold. A good starting contact.
Captain Aldric
Castle Guard
Commands the Castle Gate. Protects the borders and rewards status, honor, and loyalty.
Elder Thorn
Wise Elder
Sits in the Sacred Grove. Knows ancient lore, prophecies, and details about the shard.
Shade
Shadow Guild
Hangs in the Tavern docks. Deals in rumors, items of questionable origin, and secrecy.
